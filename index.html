How to make Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  1. 3 1/4 cups of flour
  2. 1 teaspoon baking soda
  3. 3/4 teaspoon salt
  4. 1 1/3 cups of butter, softened
  5. 1 1/4 cups of granulated sugar
  6. 1 cup of firmly packed light brown sugar
  7. 2 eggs
  8. 4 teaspoons of McCormick All Natural Pure Vanilla Extract
  9. 1 package (12 ounces) of semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°. Mix flour, baking soda and salt in medium bowl. Set aside.Beat butter and sugar in large bowl with electric mixer on medium speed until light and fluffy. Add eggs and vanilla; mix well. Gradually beat in flour mixture on low speed until well mixed. Stir in chocolate chips.
  2. Drop by rounded tablespoons about 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.
  3. Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until lightly browned. Cool on baking sheets 1 minute. Remove to wire racks; cool completely.
